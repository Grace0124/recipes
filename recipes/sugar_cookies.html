Sugar Cookies

Description

Buttery, sweet cookies. Perfect for any season.

Ingredients

  • 2.75 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1.5 cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Stir together flour, baking soda, and baking powder.
  3. Beat sugar and butter together in a separate bowl.
  4. Beat in the egg and vanilla.
  5. Add the flour mixture to the wet mixture.
  6. Roll dough into golf-sized balls.
  7. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes.
