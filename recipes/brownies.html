Brownies

Description

A higher protein, lower calorie brownie recipe.

Ingredients

  • 42g cocoa powder
  • 24g chocolate protein powder
  • 40g flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 80g sweetener
  • 220mL diet coke
  • pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients.
  3. Pour in the soda and mix until combined.
  4. Pour mix into a loaf pan lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake for 35 minutes.
